SSC-Core Interactive Topology Viewer

Pure HTML + Canvas • Offline • Loads ssc_trace.csv and renders Psi(t) and g-stream • Recomputes Omega, K, D, F deterministically
Expected CSV headers (from SSC-Core trace): W_allow_cum, Psi, g, sq_used
The viewer recomputes: m_out = sum(sq_used), W = number of rows, W_allow = last W_allow_cum, Omega = sum(1 - Psi), K = sum_{i=2..N} |g(i) - g(i-1)|, D = W_allow(N) / W(N), F = sha256(g_stream || '|' || Psi_samples || '|' || Omega || '|' || K)
Top plot: Psi(t) curve. Bottom plot: g(i) bars (0/1). Axes are deterministic and scale to your run.
